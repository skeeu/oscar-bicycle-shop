About Oscar

Oscar is a bike store and repair workshop based in Bushwick, New York. We encourage our customers to explore the urban environment in the best way they can: on two wheels. If you’re looking for a new ride, come on by for a chat. If you need repairs or a tuneup on your bike, drop by any time and we’ll be happy to help.

From city cruisers to road racers, all our bikes and custom builds are characterized by quality parts and mindful construction.
